Welcome to the Level Editor

Create your own level!

Use your mouse to place blocks. When you are done designing your level you can play and share it!

Controls

click + drag to adjust angle.

click to place a voxel.

shift to delete a voxel.

alt to bulk edit voxels.

1-5 to change color.

+/- or scroll to zoom.

A to rotate the view
